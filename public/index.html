Hello There!

I am currently working as a Sr. Assistant Professor of Computer Science Engineering at Vellore Institute of Technology, University, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. I have completed my B. Tech. in Information Technology from Women’s Institute of Technology, 2017. I did my M.Tech. (CSE) from Galgotia’s University, Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, 2019. Additionally, I completed my Ph.D. (Computational Intelligence) from Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, Tamil Nadu, in January 2023.

My Research area is Soft Computing, Data Analytics, Data Science, Bioinspired Algorithms, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Health Informatics. I am having 3 years Experience of Assistant Professor Junior at Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, from January 2020 to January 2023. I also worked for National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NACC) Committee. As of now, I published 6 papers in SCOPUS, SCI Index, Non-Paid, Impact Factor Journal, 3 Conference papers in IEEE Xplore, and 3 Book chapters in Springer. Additionally, Two Times I was awarded for RAMAN RESEARCH AWARD from the Vellore Institute of Technology, in 2022. I worked for HCL Project (Android Development Application) and V-Care (Website Development). My Achievement is I qualified NPTEL-IIT Madras (12 weeks) Course on Introduction to Machine Learning by Prof. Balaraman Ravindra in 2021. I cleared GATE in 2017 with a GATE Score of 578. My Teaching Subject Interest is Data Structure and Algorithm, Database Management System, Operating System, Data mining and Data Warehousing, Expert Systems, Fuzzy Logic, Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Python, and MATLAB.
